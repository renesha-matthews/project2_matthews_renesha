About Me

I am currently a graduate student at the University of Florida, attaining a Master of Arts in Mass Communication degree with a specialization in Web Design & Online Communication. I received my B.S. degree from The University of Alabama, where I studied Marketing and completed minor courses in Creative Media. Currently, I am residing in Atlanta, Georgia.

Since high school, I have always been intrigued by the idea of designing and developing products. After revisiting this passion, I decided to pursue a career in Web Design/Development in order to create websites which are essentially products for the web. I take a mobile-first approach and “users first” mentality when I develop websites. In the near future, I aspire to become a front-end developer and UX/UI designer.

In my spare time, I love listening to music, playing video games, reading, watching Netflix, and spending time with family.
