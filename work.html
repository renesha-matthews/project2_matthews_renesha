Travel Vida

Travel Vida is a relatively new online booking service for people who want to experience a location from a different perspective. The service focuses on exotic locations that are less traveled but still offer a variety of people, activities and fun.

For this project, I created ad campaigns for the following social media platforms: Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. Together, they present a cohesive look yet each platform is different in its own unique way.
