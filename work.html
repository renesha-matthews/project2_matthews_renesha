My Work

Advertising

Travel Vida

Travel Vida is a relatively new online booking service for people who want to experience a location from a different perspective. The service focuses on exotic locations that are less traveled but still offer a variety of people, activities and fun.

For this project, I created ad campaigns for the following social media platforms: Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. Together, they present a cohesive look yet each platform is different in its own unique way.

Branding

Gainesville Economic Development Corporation

The agency's mission is to recruit, grow and expand new businesses and industries to the Greater Gainesville Area. The agency drives new growth and development in the area by partnering with key stakeholders such as private enterprise, local and state government and workforce entities (colleges/universities, job centers).

For this project, GEDC requested a new identity that is fresh and exciting in order to attract new businesses to the area. I created three distinct logo concepts in addition to variations of each concept for flexibility.
